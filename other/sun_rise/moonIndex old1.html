Moon position programs:

Sun and Moon position simulator 1

Shows the position of the Sun and the Moon...
...any date ...any time ...any latitude (in Northern Hemisphere)
- age and phase of the Moon is shown

The sky is one colour, and that means only the illuminated portion of the moon is shown.
If you want a graduated sky choose the next sim...

Sun and Moon position simulator 2

Like the above simulation...
...but with a graduated sky,
and so moon's unilluminated side is black rather than invisible.

Sun and Moon position simulator 1 with 'big moon'

Like the first simulation...
...but with a large moon overlapped to show the phase in more detail.

Sun and Moon position simulator 2 with 'big moon'

Like the second simulation...
...but with a large moon overlapped to show the phase in more detail.

Sun and Moon position simulator with 'image'

Sort of like the above simulation...
...but the large moon attempts to show an image of the moon,
and the illuminated portion of the moon does not angle towards the Sun
