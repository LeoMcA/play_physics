Solar position programs:

Sun position simulator - Northern Hemisphere

Choose the time of day...
Choose the date...
Choose your latitude (Northern Hemisphere)...
...and see the Sun's position in the sky.
- not 100% accurate, as everything (Earth's orbit, Earth's surface etc.) is assumed to be circular;
but sunrise and sunset times seem to be +- 15 mins - but, beware, times are not 'daylight-saving-time',
so you might need to add 1 hour to sunrise/set if in summer months and your country uses DST.
