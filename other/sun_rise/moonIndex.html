Moon position programs:

Sun and Moon position simulator - rotational 'sliders'

Shows the position of the Sun and the Moon...
Choose ...any date ...any time ...any latitude (in Northern Hemisphere) - using rotational 'sliders'
(this means you can move thru top of hour/midnight/end of month/new year without needing to reset the slider)
- age and phase of the Moon is shown

There are buttons to control:

  • colour of darkside of moon
  • colour of the bright side of moon
  • colour of the sky
  • whether there is a large moon to see the phase better
  • the visual angle of the sun and moon
  • and more...

Sun and Moon position simulator - sliders

Shows the position of the Sun and the Moon...
Choose ...any date ...any time ...any latitude (in Northern Hemisphere) - using SLIDERS
- age and phase of the Moon is shown

There are similar buttons to control various aspects - as detailed above.

Sun and Moon position simulator with 'image'

Sort of like the above simulation...
...but the large moon attempts to show an image of the moon,
and the illuminated portion of the moon does not angle towards the Sun

A Moon phase simulator

Choose the date...
...choose the time of day.
...choose the date...
