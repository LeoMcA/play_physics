Jingxin Nie et al. 2019 – procedure for LTM tests:


Long-term memory for numerical figures


In this experiment, subjects were given eighteen double-digit numbers to remember 3 days prior to the experiment.


During the test, after the word “Start” was shown on the screen for 2 s,


thirteen double-digit numerical figures were shown on the screen in turn at the speed of 2 s per figure.


The subjects were asked to press the button with their right hand immediately when they thought that the displayed figure was one of the figures that were given to remember before


and do nothing if not.


In control trial, thirteen double-digit figures were shown on the screen in turn at the speed of 2 s per figure.


When the 13 double-digit figures were shown in turn, the subjects were asked to determine whether currently displayed figure is “50” or not.


The subjects were asked to press the button with their right hand if the figure “50” were shown.
