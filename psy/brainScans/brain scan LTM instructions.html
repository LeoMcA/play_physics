Long-term memory test for numeric figures


INSTRUCTIONS:


Put the 3 letter ‘word’ that is associated with the 18 numbers that you have memorised to your long-term memory.


Press ‘Submit’ button.


Press ‘GO!’ button.


The word “Start” is shown on the screen for 2 s.


13 double digit numbers are shown, one at a time, at the centre of the screen, at the speed of 2 s per number.


If you see a number that was on the original list of 18 press the ‘Yes’ button.


The answer is then displayed showing:


  • correct: how many numbers you correctly spotted

  • wrong: how many numbers you incorrectly thought had been in the original list


coding:


    • 0=no repeat – number not in the 18

    • 1=repeat missed – number in the 18 missed

    • 2=repeat found – number in the 18 found

    • 3=false positive – number not in the 18 incorrectly identified
