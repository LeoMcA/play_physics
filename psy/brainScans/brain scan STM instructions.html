Short-term memory test for numeric figures

 

INSTRUCTIONS:

 

Press ‘GO!’.

 

The word “Start turn #” is shown on the screen for 1.5 s.

 

7 single random digits (from 0 to 9) are shown, one at a time, at the centre of the screen, at the speed of 0.75 s per digit.

 

The word “Remember” is shown on the screen for 1 s,

 

Then another series of 7 single digits are shown in the same way at the same speed - one at a time, at the centre of the screen, at the speed of 0.75 s per digit.  

 

Then the instruction “Judge” is displayed on the screen for 2 s

 

During the 2 s of ‘judgement’ you must determine whether the 2nd sequence of 7 digits exactly matched (same digits in same order) the 1st sequence of 7 digits or not.

 

If the sequences MATCH you must PRESS the button marked ‘Yes’.

 

Do nothing if the sequences DO NOT match – DO NOT PRESS the button!

 

The answer is then displayed:

 

  • •‘repeat’ – the 2 sequences were the same 

  • •‘no-repeat’ – the 2 sequences were not the same 

  • •‘right’ – you identified correctly 

  • •‘wrong’ – you identified incorrectly 

 

The memory test is repeated 10 times, and then a score is displayed:

  • •how many you got correct, how many incorrect 

  • •and a score number is given using the following system - 

 

    • ◦2 points for correctly identifying a repeat (pressing ‘yes’ correctly) 

    • ◦1 point for correctly identifying a no-repeat (NOT pressing ‘yes’ correctly) 

    • ◦0 points for missing a repeat (not pressing ‘yes’ when you should have) 

    • ◦-1 point for identifying a repeat that was not a repeat (pressing ‘yes’ when you should not have)
