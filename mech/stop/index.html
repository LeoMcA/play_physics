Thinking Distance simulations:

When the simulation starts the car is already moving. You will then need to click on the scene when told to 'stop' - depending on which simulation you choose there are increasingly harder criteria for when you must stop - they require greater attention/focus.

STOP when it says 'Stop!'

STOP when first and last numbers match...

STOP when first and last numbers match or when it says 'STOP!'

STOP when 2nd and 5th numbers match...

STOP when any 2 numbers match...
