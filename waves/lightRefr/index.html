The Refraction of Light

Alter the refractive index of the 2 media.
Alter the angle of incidence.
The simulation displays the angle of refraction.

Refraction thru' a semicircular glass block

While the 1st Sim (above) can show Total Internal Reflection, this Sim is explicitly meant for showing TIR using a semicircular block as might be shown in the school lab.

Fermat's Principle of Least Time

User can change the start and end point of the light ray, and where the ray passes thru' the boundary between the 2 media.
The simulation displays the distance travelled, and the time taken, by the light on the chosen path.
Which path has the shortest distance? Which path has the smallest time? What are the angles of those paths?
