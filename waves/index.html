Light Reflection Sims - click here

Reflection: These Sims are all showing a ray of light reflecting off a mirror, showing the angles involved, and the locations of the object and image
The Law of Reflection is almost trivial - so experiments to measure angles can be rather anti-climactic...
These Sims include projected numbers which offer a range of unpredictable experiments which create unpredictable, yet continuous graphs.
Also a variant Sim is offered that measures the time taken by the light to travel, so showing how the Law of Reflection arises from Fermat's principle - the principle of least time
